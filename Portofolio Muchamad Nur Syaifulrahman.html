About Me

Where aviation meets innovation.

I am an airport management professional focused on Airport Operations, Apron Movement Control (AMC), and data-driven operational system development. With extensive experience in controlling aircraft movements, real-time apron monitoring, ground handling coordination, and terminal operational oversight, I ensure all activities are safe, efficient, timely, and in accordance with applicable aviation safety regulations. I am experienced in managing multi-stakeholder coordination involving airlines, ATC, ground handling, and other related operational units. I am also skilled at resolving operational conflicts through the development of SOPs and process improvements that enhance efficiency and safety discipline. I actively drive operational improvement and digitalization by developing AMC and Terminal reporting systems, a website-based Apron Monitoring System, a visualization of the AMC Unit KPI dashboard, and a dashboard for recording and monitoring aviation fuel expenditures for the AFS Unit. Furthermore, I contribute to airport facility improvements through interior design, creating a more modern, comfortable, and professional terminal environment. Currently, I serve as Head of the Terminal Unit, leading terminal operations with primary responsibility for improving service quality, operational safety, stakeholder coordination, and driving continuous data-driven improvement and efficiency.
